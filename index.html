Seattle's wonderland

Seattle which is also known as the Emerald city is one of the best known places for hikers with the abundant scenic locations. This part of Pacific North West comprises of so many mountain ranges, trails across lakes that would convert anyone into a nature freak. Seattle has many state parks for camping as well.

Moving to Seattle

Seattle is on of the beautiful cities to live in USA. It has been flourishing since the Microsoft, Boeing and Starbucks started growing rapidly with an era of expansion. These days we see many startups making their place in this city. This city attracts people from all over the world with an eye of cross-culture communication.
